WHAT IT DOES

One glance: GO / MAYBE / NO for your skill level, wind-and-daylight aware. Hour-by-hour and 7-day charts with tide, rain, sun and water temp. Works offline at the beach parking lot. Cross-checks 4 independent wave models and tells you when they disagree. 158 bundled spots worldwide plus live map search. Your own spot photos with camera capture. Svenska och English. Dawn-patrol dark mode. And the ocean up top? It renders the actual conditions — height, period, wind chop, water colour, real sunrise light.

THE HONEST BIT

This is a hobby app built by a surfer, not a company. It isn't on the Play Store, so Android shows scary warnings — that's normal for sideloaded apps. Forecasts come from open marine models and the app always shows you how much to trust them.
Wave data: Open-Meteo (open marine models) · Spots & coastlines: OpenStreetMap contributors
